75 day hard challenge

The 75 Hard Challenge is a mental toughness program that includes daily tasks such as two workouts, a diet, water intake, and reading. It lasts for 75 consecutive days, promoting discipline and resilience.

    • 30-Day Vegetarian or Vegan Challenge

    The 30-Day Vegetarian or Vegan Challenge encourages participants to adopt a plant-based diet for a month. It promotes health, environmental awareness, and experimenting with cruelty-free eating.

    • Intermittent Fasting Challenge

    Intermittent Fasting Challenge involves cycles of eating and fasting. Popular methods include the 16/8 (16-hour fast, 8-hour eating window). It may aid weight loss, metabolism, and overall health when practiced carefully.

  • Add
    • 21-Day No Alcohol Challenge:

    The 21-Day No Alcohol Challenge is a commitment to abstain from alcoholic beverages for three weeks. It can promote improved health, sleep, and awareness of drinking habits.

  • Add
    • The 30-Day Sleep Challenge

    The 30-Day Sleep Challenge aims to improve sleep quality by developing healthy sleep habits. Participants focus on consistent bedtime routines, proper sleep environments, and relaxation techniques to enhance restorative sleep. 

  • Add
    • 30-Day Mental Toughness Challenge

    The 30-Day Mental Toughness Challenge is a program to enhance mental resilience. It involves daily exercises and tasks designed to build grit, determination, and a stronger mindset.

  • Add
    • 30-Day Water Challenge

    The 30-Day Water Challenge encourages participants to meet a daily water intake goal. It promotes hydration, supporting overall health, energy, and well-being. Typically, individuals aim to drink at least eight 8-ounce glasses of water daily.

  • Add
    • 10,000 Steps a Day Challenge

    The 10,000 Steps a Day Challenge encourages daily physical activity by walking at least 10,000 steps, promoting cardiovascular health, weight management, and an active lifestyle. Fitness trackers are often used to track progress.

  • Add
    • 30-Day Mindfulness Meditation Challenge

    The 30-Day Mindfulness Meditation Challenge involves daily meditation practices to reduce stress, increase self-awareness, and improve mental well-being. It cultivates mindfulness, often starting with short sessions and gradually increasing duration.
