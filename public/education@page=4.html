Martial arts encompass various disciplines like karate and judo, focusing on self-defense, discipline, agility, and physical fitness. Practicing martial arts can improve confidence, coordination, and overall health.

Tennis is a racket sport that enhances agility, cardiovascular health, and hand-eye coordination. It's a fun and social way to stay active while improving physical fitness and mental focus.

Team Sports

Team sports like soccer, basketball, and volleyball promote teamwork, social interaction, and physical fitness. They improve cardiovascular health, coordination, and overall well-being through competitive play and cooperation.
