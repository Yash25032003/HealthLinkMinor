Eggs

Eggs are a highly nutritious food, rich in protein, vitamins (especially B12), and minerals. They are a versatile ingredient used in various culinary dishes and are a source of essential nutrients.

  • Add
    • Leafy greens

    Leafy greens, like spinach and kale, are rich in vitamins (e.g., A, K, C), minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. They promote overall health, aid in digestion, and may reduce the risk of chronic diseases.     

  • Add
    • Fish

    Fish is a valuable source of lean protein, omega-3 fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals. Regular consumption supports heart health, brain function, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.

  • Add
    • Cruciferous vegetables

    Cruciferous vegetables, such as broccoli, cabbage, and cauliflower, are rich in fiber, vitamins (C, K), and phytonutrients. They're known for their potential to reduce the risk of cancer and support overall health.

  • Add
    • Chicken breast and some lean meats

    Chicken breast and lean meats like turkey and lean cuts of beef are excellent sources of protein, vital for muscle growth and repair. They are low in saturated fats, making them a healthy choice for balanced diets. 

  • Add
    • Potatoes and other root vegetables

    Potatoes and root vegetables like carrots and sweet potatoes provide essential carbohydrates, fiber, vitamins (e.g., C, A), and minerals. They are versatile, nutritious staples in many diets, offering sustained energy and promoting overall health.

  • Add
    • Beans and legumes

    Beans and legumes, such as lentils and chickpeas, are plant-based sources of protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. They support heart health, digestion, and can be a valuable component of vegetarian and vegan diets.

  • Add
    • Soups

    Soups are versatile dishes made by simmering ingredients like vegetables, meats, or legumes in a liquid base. They provide a nutritious and comforting meal, often rich in vitamins, minerals, and hydration.

  • Add
    • Cottage cheese

    Cottage cheese is a dairy product known for its high protein content and low fat. It's a versatile ingredient in cooking and offers essential nutrients like calcium and B vitamins, supporting muscle growth and overall health.
